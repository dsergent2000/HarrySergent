“Family” Harry’s father, Albert Edwin Sergent-Shadbolt (my Great Grandfather) was born 4th July 1863 in Radford, Nottinghamshire, England. At the age of 14 he immigrated with his family to New Zealand, aboard the ship “Wairoa”. Harry’s mother, Martha Elizabeth “Lizzie” Pyne was born in Poplar, London in 1871. By the time she was 4 years old her family immigrated to New Zealand.
After marrying, Martha and Albert had four children. May Ellen Sergent-Shadbolt 1897-1981 Elsie Jane Sergent-Shadbolt 1895-1976 Albert William Sergent-Shadbolt 1911-2005 Henry ‘Harry’ Sergent-Shadbolt 1914-2010 Harry’s father Albert supported them by working as a pig farmer in Okola. Our family surname name prior to the 1900’s was ‘Sergent-Shadbolt’, but Shadbolt was dropped at a later date by the Australian side of the family. Whilst working for Imperial Airways (the predecessor of British Airways), Harry met my Grandmother, Renee Saul in Egypt. They married and went on to have 3 children: Clive who was born in New Zealand, Brian who was born in England & Allan who was born in Egypt. Harry spent the last 30 years of his life happily married to Daphne Sergent. Daphne’s son, Roger, assisted greatly with the safe keeping of Harry’s aviation documents.
